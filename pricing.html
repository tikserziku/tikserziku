Common questions

Do I need to pay for server infrastructure?
No. Every plan includes a dedicated virtual machine — you don't pay for infrastructure separately. We handle provisioning, monitoring, and maintenance. Your agents use multiple AI providers for redundancy and speed, with over 19,000 requests per day included.
Is my data private?
Yes. Each customer gets their own dedicated VM. Your data never leaves your server. We don't have access to your files, conversations, or generated content. You own everything.
What can 8 agents actually do?
Research topics in parallel, write code, generate content, create images, analyze data, produce Google Docs/Sheets/Slides, and more — all simultaneously. Give one task, get results from multiple specialized agents working together.
Can I customize the agents?
On Team and Business plans, yes. You can change agent roles, system prompts, preferred models, and create entirely custom workflows. Power-Ups let you add new capabilities.
What happens if a service crashes?
The autonomous agent (watchdog) monitors all services and auto-restarts crashed processes. It learns from failures and applies fixes. Your swarm self-heals.
How do I interact with my agents?
Via Telegram bot (natural language), web dashboard, or API. Send a message like "research competitors in AI market" and the swarm decomposes, parallelizes, and delivers results.
